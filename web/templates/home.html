Getting started

Three steps.
That's all it takes.

No bank branch. No credit history. No paperwork. Just your phone.

Sign up in 30 seconds

Your phone number is your account. No ID required to get started. Higher limits unlock with optional verification.

Deposit via M-Pesa

Send from M-Pesa to your YeboBank wallet. Your KES converts to satoshis instantly at the live Bitcoin rate.

Save, earn, and send

Lock sats to earn interest. Join your chama. Send to any phone or Lightning wallet in the world — instantly.

What you get

A real bank.
Not just a wallet.

Every feature built for how people in Kenya actually live and save.

01

Interest-bearing savings

Lock your sats for 30 days and earn 5% APY, paid every month. Interest comes from transparent treasury yield — never from other users' deposits.

→ Savings accounts
02

Chama group wallets

Your community savings group, on Bitcoin. Create a chama, invite members, collect contributions, and vote on payouts — all on-chain and transparent.

→ Group savings
03

Global Lightning payments

Send sats to anyone in the world in under a second via the Lightning Network. No SWIFT. No correspondent banks. No 3-day wait. No $35 fee.

→ Send worldwide
04

M-Pesa cash network

Deposit and withdraw using M-Pesa — the network 51 million Kenyans already trust. No new app to download, no new habit to learn.

→ M-Pesa in/out
05

Agent cash network

Walk into any YeboBank agent — a local shop, market stall, or kiosk — and cash in or out. No smartphone needed. No internet required.

→ Local agents
06

Fully open source

Every line of code is public. MIT licensed. Community-owned. You don't have to trust us — you can read the code that holds your money.
